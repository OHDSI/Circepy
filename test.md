### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:
1. condition occurrences of 'Immune Thrombocytopenia'.

### Inclusion Criteria
#### 1. No congenital or genetic thrombocytopenia
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Congenital or genetic causes for thrombocytopenia', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
#### 2. No Platelet count > 100 on index date
Entry events having no measurements of 'Platelet measurement', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 101 and 450; unit: "thousand per microliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter", "billion per liter", "million per milliliter" or "billion cells per liter".
#### 3. No thrombocytosis on index date
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Thrombocytosis', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
#### 4. No Pancytopenia or bone marrow disorder diagnosis within 7 days
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Pancytopenia & bone marrow disorder', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
#### 5. No Neutropenia, Agranulocytosis diagnosis within 7 days
Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Neutropenia, Agranulocytosis or Unspecified Leukopenia', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
#### 6. No low neutrophil count within 7 days
Entry events with all of the following criteria:
    1. having no measurements of 'Neutrophil Absolute Count', starting between 7 days before and 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 0.01 and 1.499; unit: "billion per liter", "thousand per microliter", "million per milliliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter" or "billion cells per liter"; low range between 1.5 and 4.
    2. having no measurements of 'Neutrophil Absolute Count', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 10 and 1,500; unit: "cells per microliter" or "per microliter".
#### 7. No Anemia diagnosis within 7 days
Entry events with all of the following criteria:
    1. having no condition occurrences of 'Anemia or Reticulocytopenia', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
    2. having no observations of 'Anemia or Reticulocytopenia', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
#### 8. No low Hemoglobin measurement in blood within 7 days
Entry events having no measurements of 'Hemoglobin measurement', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 4 and 11; unit: "g/dl", "gram per deciliter" or "gram per deciliter calculated".
### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.
The person exits the cohort when encountering any of the following events:
1. measurements of 'Platelet measurement', numeric value between 150 and 450; unit: "thousand per microliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter", "billion per liter", "million per milliliter" or "billion cells per liter".
2. condition occurrences of 'Thrombocytosis'.
### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
