### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'T cell malignancies_update'.

2. condition occurrences of '

Neoplasm of uncertain behavior of skin'.

3. observations of 'T cell malignancies_update'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. T cell malignancies 

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'T cell malignancies_update', starting between 0 days before and 60 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 observation of 'T cell malignancies_update', starting between 0 days before and 60 days after cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
