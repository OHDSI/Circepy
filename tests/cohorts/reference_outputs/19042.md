### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Cardiac death'.

2. observations of 'Cardiac death'.

3. condition occurrences of 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 death of any form, starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Death', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having at least 1 observation of 'Death', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.


4. observations of 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 death of any form, starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Death', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having at least 1 observation of 'Death', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.


5. procedure occurrences of 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 death of any form, starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Death', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having at least 1 observation of 'Death', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after 'Cardiac arrest (from PL, removing death concepts)' start date; allow events outside observation period.


6. condition occurrences of 'Unstable angina'; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or inpatient ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Unstable angina' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Unstable angina' start date.

7. condition occurrences of 'Myocardial Infarction and complication (PL definition)'; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or inpatient ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Myocardial Infarction and complication (PL definition)' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Myocardial Infarction and complication (PL definition)' start date.

8. condition occurrences of 'Congestive heart failure (left and congestive right)'; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or inpatient ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Congestive heart failure (left and congestive right)' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Congestive heart failure (left and congestive right)' start date.

9. condition occurrences of 'Cerebral infarction (non-hemorrhagic stroke)'; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or inpatient ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Cerebral infarction (non-hemorrhagic stroke)' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Cerebral infarction (non-hemorrhagic stroke)' start date.

10. condition occurrences of 'Hemorrhagic stroke_updated 2023'; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or inpatient ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Hemorrhagic stroke_updated 2023' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Hemorrhagic stroke_updated 2023' start date.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
