### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Long QT syndrome or QT prolonged'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Washout period - 180 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Long QT syndrome or QT prolonged', starting in the 180 days prior to cohort entry start date.

#### 2. Has No Congenital QT Syndrome

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Congenital forms of QT syndrome', allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
