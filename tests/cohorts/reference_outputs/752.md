### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Firearm accident'.

2. observations of 'Firearm accident'.

3. procedure occurrences of 'Firearm accident'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. with an ER or IP visit on index

Entry events having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or Inpatient/ER visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
