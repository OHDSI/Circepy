### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of 'Mechanical ventilation'.

2. measurements of 'Mechanical ventilation'.

3. observations of 'Mechanical ventilation'.

4. procedure occurrences of 'tracheostomy'.

5. procedure occurrences of 'Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) procedure'.

Restrict entry events to having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient Visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.  

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
