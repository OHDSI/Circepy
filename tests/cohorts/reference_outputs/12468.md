### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Headache -dk and Aura'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Migraine excluding abdominal migraine including history '.

3. drug exposures of 'Antimigraine_approved and off-label'.

4. observations of 'Migraine excluding abdominal migraine including history '.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Migraine 

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Migraine excluding abdominal migraine including history ', starting between 0 days before and 60 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 observation of 'Migraine excluding abdominal migraine including history ', starting between 0 days before and 60 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.


Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
