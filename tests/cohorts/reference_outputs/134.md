### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)'.

3. drug exposures of 'Drugs for Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has at least one condition record of ADHD

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)', starting between 0 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
