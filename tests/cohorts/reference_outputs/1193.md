### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Type 1 diabetes mellitus'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. no Type 2 diabetes mellitus specific diagnosis on or prior to T1DM

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Type 2 diabetes mellitus (T2DM-specific concepts)', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 2. no secondary diabetes diagnosis on or prior to T1DM

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Secondary diabetes mellitus', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
