### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)'; with any of the following criteria:


   1. with any of the following criteria:

      1. having at least 1 observation of 'Inpatient Care Concepts', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
      2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Inpatient Care Concepts', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
      3. having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or ER Visit', starting anytime on or before 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.

   2. with all of the following criteria:

      1. having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Outpatient Visit', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.

      2. with any of the following criteria:

         1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Bronchospasm', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
         2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Stridor', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
         3. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Hypotension', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
         4. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Epinephrine', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
         5. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Epinephrine', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
         6. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Diphenhydramine injection', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
         7. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Diphenhydramine injection', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.
         8. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation CPR', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)' start date.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Washout period 30 days - applied only to outpatient visit

Entry events having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Outpatient Visit', starting between 30 days before and 1 days after cohort entry start date; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Outpatient Visit' start date.
   2. having at least 1 observation of 'Anaphylaxis (criterion 1 or 2)', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Outpatient Visit' start date.


#### 2. No Food anaphylaxis on index date

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Food anaphylaxis', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 3. No anaphylaxis due to substances other than food or drug on index date

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Anaphylaxis due to substance', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 4. No anaphylaxis due insect bite on index date

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Anaphylaxis caused by insect bite and/or insect sting', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 5. No insect bite or sting

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Insect bite or sting', starting between 1 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no observations of 'Insect bite or sting', starting between 1 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.


#### 6. No Toxic effect of venom

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Toxic effect of venom', starting between 1 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 7. Accidental poisoning

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Accidental poisoning', starting between 1 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
