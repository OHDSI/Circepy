### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. death of any form.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

Restrict entry events to with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[LEGEND HTN] Acute myocardial Infarction', starting between 30 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[LEGEND HTN] Ischemic stroke', starting between 30 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.
   3. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[LEGEND HTN] intracranial bleed Hemorrhagic stroke', starting between 30 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.
   4. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[LEGEND HTN] Sudden cardiac death', starting between 30 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.
   5. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[LEGEND HTN] Heart Failure ', starting between 30 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or ER visit', starting anytime on or before '[LEGEND HTN] Heart Failure ' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after '[LEGEND HTN] Heart Failure ' start date.
  

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
