### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Microangiopathy'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Microangiopathy symptoms'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Microangiopathy', starting between 0 days after and 30 days after 'Microangiopathy symptoms' start date.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has no events in prior 'clean window' - 365 days

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Microangiopathy', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no condition occurrences of 'Microangiopathy symptoms', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Microangiopathy', starting between 0 days after and 30 days after 'Microangiopathy symptoms' start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
