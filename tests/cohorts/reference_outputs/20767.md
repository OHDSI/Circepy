### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Cardiac arrhythmia composite not including AF'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers'; having no condition occurrences of '

AF', starting anytime on or before 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers' start date; allow events outside observation period.

3. device exposures of 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers'; having no condition occurrences of '

AF', starting anytime on or before 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers' start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. washout out of 365

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Cardiac arrhythmia composite not including AF', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no device exposures of 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; having no condition occurrences of '

AF', starting anytime on or before 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having no procedure occurrences of 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; having no condition occurrences of '

AF', starting anytime on or before 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers' start date; allow events outside observation period.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
