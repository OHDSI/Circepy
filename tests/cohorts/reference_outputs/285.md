### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Myocarditis Pericarditis'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Remove subjects with Infectious Myocarditis or Pericarditis

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Infectious Etiology for Myopericarditis', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 90 days of each other.
