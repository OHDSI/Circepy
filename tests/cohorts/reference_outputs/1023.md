### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Major Depressive Disorder' for the first time in the person's history; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Suicide and suicidal ideation, prevalent (informed by PHOEBE)', starting between 365 days before and 0 days after 'Major Depressive Disorder' start date.
   2. having at least 1 observation of 'Suicide and suicidal ideation, prevalent (informed by PHOEBE)', starting between 365 days before and 0 days after 'Major Depressive Disorder' start date.


2. condition occurrence of 'Suicide and suicidal ideation, prevalent (informed by PHOEBE)' for the first time in the person's history; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Major Depressive Disorder', starting between 365 days before and 0 days after 'Suicide and suicidal ideation, prevalent (informed by PHOEBE)' start date.

3. observation of 'Suicide and suicidal ideation, prevalent (informed by PHOEBE)' for the first time in the person's history; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Major Depressive Disorder', starting between 365 days before and 0 days after 'Suicide and suicidal ideation, prevalent (informed by PHOEBE)' start date.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
