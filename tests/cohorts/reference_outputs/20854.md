### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Thrombocytopenia (broad)'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No measurements in normal range on same day

Entry events having no measurements of 'Platelet volume measurement', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value between 151 and 600; unit: "thousand per microliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter", "per cubic millimeter", "cubic millimeter", "billion per liter", "per liter" or "million per milliliter".

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 90 days of each other.
