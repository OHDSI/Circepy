### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. observation of 'Death' for the first time in the person's history.

2. death of any form.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Overdose

Entry events having at least 1 measurement of 'Evidence of tests for overdose', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
