### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Major depressive disorder' for the first time in the person's history.

2. observation of 'Major depressive disorder' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

Restrict entry events to with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Bipolar disorder', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.
   2. having no condition occurrences of 'Schizoaffective disorder', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.
   3. having no condition occurrences of 'Schizophrenia not including Paraphenia', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.
   4. having no condition occurrences of 'Dementia', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.
   5. having no condition occurrences of 'Psychotic disorder', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.
  

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
