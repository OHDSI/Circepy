### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 365 days before event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'PTSD [post-traumatic stress disorder]' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. prior psychoses

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[PheValuator] Schizophrenia not including Paraphenia', starting anytime up to 30 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[PheValuator] Schizoaffective disorder', starting anytime up to 30 days after cohort entry start date.
   3. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Psychotic disorder', starting anytime up to 30 days after cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
