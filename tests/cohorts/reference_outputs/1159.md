### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Angina'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. no Angina in prior 30 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Angina', starting in the 30 days prior to cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 1 days of each other.
