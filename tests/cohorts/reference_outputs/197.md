### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Coronary artery disease '.

2. observations of 'Coronary artery disease '.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 99,999 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
