### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Lymphopenia Conditions'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No Lymphocytes normal or above the range >1

Entry events having no measurements of 'Lymphocytopenia Measurements - absolute counts in blood', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value between 1 and 40; unit: "billion per liter" or "thousand per microliter".

#### 2. No Lymphocytes normal or above the range >1000

Entry events having no measurements of 'Lymphocytopenia Measurements - absolute counts in blood', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value between 1,000 and 40,000; unit: "cells per microliter" or "thousand per cubic millimeter".

#### 3. No Lymphocytosis or lymphoid leukemia

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Lymphocytosis or lymphoid leukemia', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 183 days of each other.
