### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Immune Thrombocytopenia'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. 365 days  washout period 

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Immune Thrombocytopenia', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date.

#### 2. No congenital or genetic thrombocytopenia

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Congenital or genetic causes for thrombocytopenia', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 3. No Platelet count > 100 on index date

Entry events having no measurements of 'Platelet measurement', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 101 and 450; unit: "thousand per microliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter", "billion per liter", "million per milliliter" or "billion cells per liter".

#### 4. No thrombocytosis on index date

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Thrombocytosis', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 5. No Hypersplenism

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hypersplenism', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 6. No Antiphospholipid syndrome

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Antiphospholipid syndrome', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 7. No Paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 8. No Hemolytic uremic syndrome

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hemolytic uremic syndrome', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 9. No Thrombotic microangiopathy

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Thrombotic microangiopathy', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 10. No major autoimmune disorders (RA, MS, Psoriasis, SLE, CD, UC, Addisons, Graves, Sjogren, Myasthenia Gravis, Vasculitis, Celiac)

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'No major autoimmune disorders (RA, MS, Psoriasis, SLE, CD, UC, Addisons, Graves, Sjogren, Myasthenia Gravis, Vasculitis, Celiac)', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 11. No Chronic Liver Disease

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Chronic Liver Disease', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 12. No Pregnancy related HELLP syndrome (in past 3 months)

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'HELLP syndrome', starting between 90 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 13. No tumors of hematopoietic cells including lymphoma, leukemia, myeloma and related

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Tumors of hematopoietic cells including lymphoma, leukemia, myeloma and related', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no observations of 'Tumors of hematopoietic cells including lymphoma, leukemia, myeloma and related', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.


#### 14. No Nutritional deficiency

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Nutritional deficiency (mostly malnutrition)', starting anytime up to 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 15. No Pancytopenia or bone marrow disorder diagnosis within 7 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Pancytopenia & bone marrow disorder', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 16. No Neutropenia, Agranulocytosis diagnosis within 7 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Neutropenia, Agranulocytosis or Leukopenia', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 17. No low neutrophil count within 7 days

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no measurements of 'Neutrophil Absolute Count', starting between 7 days before and 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 0.01 and 1.499; unit: "billion per liter", "thousand per microliter", "million per milliliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter" or "billion cells per liter"; low range between 1.5 and 4.
   2. having no measurements of 'Neutrophil Absolute Count', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 10 and 1,500; unit: "cells per microliter" or "per microliter".
   3. having at least 1 measurement of 'Neutrophil Absolute Count', with value as concept: "below reference range".


#### 18. No Anemia diagnosis within 7 days

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Anemia or Reticulocytopenia', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no observations of 'Anemia or Reticulocytopenia', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.


#### 19. No low Hemoglobin measurement in blood within 7 days

Entry events having no measurements of 'Hemoglobin measurement', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 4 and 11; unit: "g/dl", "gram per deciliter" or "gram per deciliter calculated".

#### 20. No low Hematocrit measurement in blood within 7 days

Entry events having no measurements of 'Hematocrit measurement', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; unit: "percent".

#### 21. No Pancytopenia or bone marrow disorder Observation within 7 days

Entry events having no observations of 'Pancytopenia & bone marrow disorder', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 180 days.
The person exits the cohort when encountering any of the following events:
1. measurements of 'Platelet measurement', numeric value between 150 and 450; unit: "thousand per microliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter", "billion per liter", "million per milliliter" or "billion cells per liter".
2. condition occurrences of 'Thrombocytosis'.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
