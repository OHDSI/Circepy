### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Neurofibromatosis syndrome'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. >=3 Ophthalmology Visits: anytime in persons history, three visits within one year  

Entry events having at least 1 visit occurrence of any visit, a provider specialty that is: "ophthalmology"; having at least 2 distinct start dates from visit occurrences of any visit, starting between 1 days after and 365 days after any visit start date; a provider specialty that is: "ophthalmology".

#### 2. MRI of Brain: anytime in persons history  

Entry events having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'MRI of brain'.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
