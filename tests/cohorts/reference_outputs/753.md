### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. observations of 'Motor vehicle accident'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Motor vehicle accident'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. with an ER or IP visit 

Entry events having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'ER or IP  visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 180 days of each other.
