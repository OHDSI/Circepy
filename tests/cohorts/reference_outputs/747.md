### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

Restrict entry events to with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Right Heart Catheterization', starting anytime up to 30 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Echocardiogram', starting anytime up to 30 days after cohort entry start date.
  

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
