### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of '[LEGEND HTN] Heart Failure '.

Restrict entry events to having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[LEGEND HTN] Heart Failure ', starting between 1 days after and 365 days after cohort entry start date.  

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 1 days of each other.
