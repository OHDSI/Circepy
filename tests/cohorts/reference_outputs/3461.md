### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Lupus nephritis' for the first time in the person's history.

2. condition occurrence of 'Renal Disease' for the first time in the person's history; having at least 2 condition occurrences of 'Systemic lupus erythematosus', starting anytime on or before 'Renal Disease' start date.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
