### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 365 days before event enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Malignant melanoma'; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or inpatient ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Malignant melanoma' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Malignant melanoma' start date.

2. condition occurrences of 'Malignant melanoma'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Malignant melanoma', starting between 1 days after and 30 days after 'Malignant melanoma' start date.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
