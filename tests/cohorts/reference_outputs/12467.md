### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Headache -dk and Aura'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Migraine excluding abdominal migraine' for the first time in the person's history, starting between 0 days before and 60 days after 'Headache -dk and Aura' start date; allow events outside observation period.

2. condition occurrence of 'Migraine excluding abdominal migraine' for the first time in the person's history.

3. drug exposures of 'Antimigraine'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Migraine excluding abdominal migraine' for the first time in the person's history, starting between 0 days before and 60 days after 'Antimigraine' start date; allow events outside observation period.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
