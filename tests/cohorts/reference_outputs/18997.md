### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of '[EPI_1195] Hyperlipidemia (excl Disorder of lipid metabolism concept)' for the first time in the person's history.

2. drug exposure of 'lipid lowering agents' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Has a hyperlipidemia diagnosis 0 to 60d after entry 

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[EPI_1195] Hyperlipidemia (excl Disorder of lipid metabolism concept)', starting between 0 days before and 60 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. Has 365d prior continuous enrollment

Entry events having at least 1 observation period, starting anytime up to 365 days before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
