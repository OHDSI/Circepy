### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Acute bronchitis'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. no acute bronchitis in prior 30d

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Acute bronchitis', starting in the 30 days prior to cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
