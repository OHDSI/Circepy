### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Anemia in chronic kidney disease'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Anemia'.

3. measurements of 'Hemoglobin measurement in blood (comprehensive set)', with value as concept: "below reference range".

4. measurements of 'Hemoglobin measurement in blood (comprehensive set)', numeric value between 1 and 7.4; unit: "mmol/l" or "millimole per liter".

5. measurements of 'Hemoglobin measurement in blood (comprehensive set)', numeric value between 1 and 12; unit: "g/dl" or "gram per deciliter".

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. CKD dx within a year prior: adding pre-coordinated term so it satisfies the criteria even w/o separate CKD code  

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[ASSURE] Chronic kidney disease', starting anytime up to 30 days after cohort entry start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
