### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Suicide and suicidal ideation (informed by PHOEBE)'.

2. observations of 'Suicide and suicidal ideation (informed by PHOEBE)'.

3. condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI_829] Suicide (ICD-9 source-codes from Coren et al., 2017)' source concepts).

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 1 days of each other.
