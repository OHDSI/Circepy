### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Acute Typical Pneumonia'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Exclude if concomitant non acute pneumonia

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Non Acute Typical Pneumonia', starting in the 1 days prior to cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
