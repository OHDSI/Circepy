### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. measurements of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', with value as concept: "detected", "positive", "positive", "present", "present" or "detected".

2. measurements of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', with value as concept: "negative", "not detected", "not detected in pooled specimen", "absent", "negative", "not detected" or "absent".

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Have atleast 1 positive

Entry events having at least 1 measurement of any measurement, starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; with value as concept: "detected", "positive", "positive", "present", "present" or "detected".

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
