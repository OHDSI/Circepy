### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Allergic rhinitis'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Rhinitis'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Allergic disorder of respiratory tract', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Rhinitis' start date.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Allergic disorder', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Rhinitis' start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 90 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
