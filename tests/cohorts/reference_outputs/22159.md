### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] Gastritis'.

2. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] Chronic Gastritis'.

3. condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] Gastritis' source concepts).

4. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] Infectious Gastritis v2'.

5. condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] Infectious Gastritis v2' source concepts).

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Exclude ICD10 codes on index 

Entry events having no condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] ICD10 exclusions Gastritis' source concepts), starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
