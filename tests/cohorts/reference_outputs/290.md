### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Thyroiditis including hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism'.

2. drug exposures of '

levothyroxine'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Thyroiditis including hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Thyroiditis including hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism', starting between 0 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. Excluding alternative causes of hypothyroidism

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hypothyroidism other than Hashimoto's Disease', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 365 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
