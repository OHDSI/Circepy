### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Primary Lung Cancer'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Another neoplasm of lung code within 365 days

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Primary Lung Cancer', starting between 1 days after and 365 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. SCLC drugs exclusion

Entry events having at most 0 drug exposures of 'SCLC only drugs', starting anytime up to 365 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 3. SCLC diagnosis exclusion 

Entry events having at most 0 condition occurrences of 'SCLC condition', starting anytime up to 365 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 4. No Upper respiratory tract Neoplasm

Entry events having no condition occurrences of '

Malignant neoplasm of upper respiratory tract', starting anytime up to 365 days after cohort entry start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
