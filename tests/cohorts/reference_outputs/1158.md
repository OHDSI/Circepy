### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. drug exposures of 'Assure Exposure - C - aspirin'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be based on a continuous exposure to 'Assure Exposure - C - aspirin':
allowing 30 days between exposures, adding 0 days after exposure ends, and using days supply and exposure end date for exposure duration.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
