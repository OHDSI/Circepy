### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. visit occurrences of 'Inpatient or ER visit'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[LEGEND HTN] Preinfarction syndrome', starting between 0 days before and all days after 'Inpatient or ER visit' start date and starting anytime on or before 'Inpatient or ER visit' end date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 7 days of each other.
