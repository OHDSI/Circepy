### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Malignant neoplasm of pancreas'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. 2nd Specific event or diagnosis

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Malignant neoplasm of pancreas', starting between 1 days after and 365 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'removal of pancreas', starting between 0 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having at least 1 measurement of 'Pancreatic cancer biomarkers', starting between 30 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value &gt;= 37; unit: "unit per milliliter".
   4. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Pancreatic cancer chemo', starting between 0 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.


Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
