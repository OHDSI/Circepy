### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Chronic kidney disease'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

Restrict entry events to with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Chronic kidney disease', starting  30 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 2 procedure occurrences of 'Dialysis', starting between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.
   3. having at least 2 observations of 'Dialysis', starting between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.
   4. having at least 2 condition occurrences of 'Dialysis', starting between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.
  

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
