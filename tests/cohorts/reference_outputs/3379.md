### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Bipolar or manic disorder (validated from Castro et al., 2015) - OPTIMIZED' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Treated 

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Antipsychotic drugs-rxnorm', starting between 365 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 drug exposure of '[RWE CTF] Anticonvulsants/Antiepileptics', starting between 365 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date.
   3. having at least 1 drug exposure of '[REWARD] Antidepressants', starting between 365 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
