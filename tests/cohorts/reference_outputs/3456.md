### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Inclusion body myositis ​symptom'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Inclusion body myositis' for the first time in the person's history, starting between 0 days before and 30 days after 'Inclusion body myositis ​symptom' start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
