### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Gaucher Disease 
'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Lipid storage disease '.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Have a Gaucher Disease 

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Gaucher Disease 
', starting between 0 days before and 90 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
