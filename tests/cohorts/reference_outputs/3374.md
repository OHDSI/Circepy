### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. drug eras of 'Epilepsy Medications for Treatment-Resistant Epilepsy Definition'; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 3 distinct standard concepts from drug eras of 'Epilepsy Medications for Treatment-Resistant Epilepsy Definition', starting anytime on or before 'Epilepsy Medications for Treatment-Resistant Epilepsy Definition' start date and ending  365 days before 'Epilepsy Medications for Treatment-Resistant Epilepsy Definition' start date.
   2. having at least 2 distinct standard concepts from drug eras of 'Epilepsy Medications for Treatment-Resistant Epilepsy Definition', starting anytime prior to 'Epilepsy Medications for Treatment-Resistant Epilepsy Definition' start date and ending  365 days before 'Epilepsy Medications for Treatment-Resistant Epilepsy Definition' start date.


Restrict entry events to having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[PL 380378000] Epilepsy Seizures excl Alcohol withdrawal-induced convulsion', starting between 365 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date.  

Limit these restricted entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
