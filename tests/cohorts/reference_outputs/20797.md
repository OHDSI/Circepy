### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Hyperglycemia'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. no diabetes in prior 365d

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Diabetes and complications', starting between 365 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. no hyperglycemic events in prior 365d

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hyperglycemia', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date.

#### 3. no conflicting glucose measure on same day

Entry events having no measurements of 'Hyperglycemia', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value between 1 and 124; unit: "milligram per deciliter".

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
