### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of 'Opioid Use Disorder - treatment procedure'.

2. observations of 'Opioid Use Disorder - treatment procedure'.

3. drug exposures of 'Opioid Use Disorder - treatment drug'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 90 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 365 days of each other.
