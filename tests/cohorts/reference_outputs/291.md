### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'gynocomastia diagnosis '.

2. procedure occurrences of 'gynocomastia procedures '.

3. condition occurrences of 'gynocomastia related presentation- breast lump and pain '.

4. observations of 'gynocomastia related presentation- breast lump and pain '.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Require Gynecomastia diagnosis 

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'gynocomastia diagnosis ', starting between 0 days after and 365 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 365 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
