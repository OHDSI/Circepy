### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'HIV excluding HIV-II  '.

Restrict entry events to with at least 1 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'HIV excluding HIV-II  ', starting  1 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'HIV drugs', starting between 0 days after and all days after cohort entry start date.
   3. having at least 1 measurement of 'HIV Screening Test', starting between 0 days after and all days after cohort entry start date; numeric value &gt; 1.
  

Limit these restricted entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
