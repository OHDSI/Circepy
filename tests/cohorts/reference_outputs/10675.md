### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Facial palsy'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has no events in prior 'clean window' 183 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Facial palsy', starting in the 183 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 2. Remove congenital Facial Palsy

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Congential Facial Palsy'.

#### 3. Not Upper Motor Neuron

Entry events having no condition occurrence of 'Stroke, cerebrovascular accident' for the first time in the person's history, starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 180 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
