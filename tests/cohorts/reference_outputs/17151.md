### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding - excl. ulcers wo explicit ref to bleeding '.

2. procedure occurrences of 'Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding - excl. ulcers wo explicit ref to bleeding '.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 30 days of each other.
