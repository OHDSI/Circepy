### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. observations of 'pattern of use causing harm or distress'.

2. condition occurrences of 'pattern of use causing harm or distress'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
