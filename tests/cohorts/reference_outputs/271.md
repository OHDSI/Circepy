### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Hemolytic Anemia'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Anemia or Reticulocytopenia that can potentially be due to hemolysis'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Hemolytic disorder', starting between 0 days after and 30 days after 'Anemia or Reticulocytopenia that can potentially be due to hemolysis' start date; allow events outside observation period.

3. measurements of 'Hemoglobin measurement', numeric value between 4 and 11; unit: "g/dl", "gram per deciliter" or "gram per deciliter calculated"; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Hemolytic disorder', starting between 0 days after and 30 days after 'Hemoglobin measurement' start date; allow events outside observation period.

4. measurements of 'Hematocrit measurement', numeric value between 5 and 30; unit: "percent"; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Hemolytic disorder', starting between 0 days after and 30 days after 'Hematocrit measurement' start date; allow events outside observation period.

Restrict entry events to with at least 1 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 2 distinct start dates from condition occurrences of 'Intracorpuscular disease', starting anytime up to 60 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
  

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 99,999 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
