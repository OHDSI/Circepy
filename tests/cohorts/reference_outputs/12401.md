### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of '[ASSURE] Atrial fibrillation'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No prior valve-related diagnosis

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Mitral stenosis or transplanted valve', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

#### 2. No prior valve-related procedure

Entry events having no procedure occurrences of '[ASSURE] Heart Valve Repair or Replacement Procedure', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
