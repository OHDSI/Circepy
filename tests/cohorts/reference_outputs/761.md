### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension' for the first time in the person's history; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'PH RHC', starting anytime up to 30 days after 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension' start date.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'PH Echocard', starting anytime up to 30 days after 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension' start date.
   3. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Left Heart Disease (for PH group 2)', starting anytime on or before 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension' start date.


2. condition occurrences of 'Left Heart Disease (for PH group 2)'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension', starting anytime on or before 'Left Heart Disease (for PH group 2)' start date; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'PH Echocard', starting anytime up to 30 days after 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension' start date.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'PH RHC', starting anytime up to 30 days after 'Pulmonary arterial hypertension' start date.


Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
