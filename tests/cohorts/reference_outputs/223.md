### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of '


Posterior reversible encephalopathy


'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Does NOT have hypertensive encephalopathy at baseline 

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'hypertensive encephalopathy ', starting between 365 days before and 1 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. Does NOT have Eclampsia at baseline 

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Eclampsia ', starting between 365 days before and 1 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
