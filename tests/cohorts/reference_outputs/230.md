### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Autoimmune hepatitis'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has no certain chronic liver disease events in prior 0 - 365 days: Exclusion of diseases that have a similar presentation, particularly viral hepatitis, drug-induced liver injury, and alcoholic liver disease  

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Certain Chronic Liver Disease', starting between 365 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 2. has no Systemic Lupus Erythematosus events in prior 0 - 365 days: Exclusion of diseases that have a similar presentation  

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Systemic Lupus Erythematosus', starting between 365 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 365 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 365 days of each other.
