### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. measurements of 'BMI measurement', numeric value between 30 and 60.

2. condition occurrences of 'Obesity diagnoses'.

3. observations of 'Obesity diagnoses'.

4. measurements of 'Body weight', numeric value between 120 and 200; unit: "kilogram".

5. observations of 'Body weight', numeric value between 265 and 440; unit: "pound".

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 365 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
