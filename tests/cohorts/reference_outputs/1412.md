### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. drug exposures of 'naloxone dispensing'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be based on a continuous exposure to 'naloxone dispensing':
allowing 1 days between exposures, adding 1 day after exposure ends, and using days supply and exposure end date for exposure duration.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
