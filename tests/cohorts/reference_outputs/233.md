### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Hemorrhagic stroke'.

Restrict entry events to having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or Inpatient/ER visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.  

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has no events in prior 'clean window' - 180 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hemorrhagic stroke', starting in the 180 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or Inpatient/ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Hemorrhagic stroke' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Hemorrhagic stroke' start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 90 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
