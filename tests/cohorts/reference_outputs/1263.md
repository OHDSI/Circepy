### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of ' Chronic obstructive lung disease'.

2. drug exposures of 'COPD combo-therapy
', who are &gt;= 55 years old.

3. drug exposures of 'COPD mono-therapy
', who are &gt;= 55 years old.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No prior asthma

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Asthma', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.
   2. having no observations of 'Asthma', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
