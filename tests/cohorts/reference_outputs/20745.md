### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of ' PH group 3 and general PH'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Minimum observation period of 365 days

Entry events having at least 1 observation period, starting anytime up to 365 days after cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. Must have Chronic Lung Disease : within last 365 days  

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of ' Chronic Lung Disease (for PH group 3)', starting between 365 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 observation of 'History of ', starting between 365 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; with value as concept: "history of chronic lung disease".


#### 3. Echocardiography or RHC: must have undergone RHC or Echo  

Entry events having at least 1 procedure occurrence of '[CTEPH] Echocardiography & RHC', starting between 365 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
