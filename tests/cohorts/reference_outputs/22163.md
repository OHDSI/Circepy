### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] GI ulcer no malignancy'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. excluded icd10 concepts on index

Entry events having no condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] Excluded icd10 codes for GI ulcer' source concepts), starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
