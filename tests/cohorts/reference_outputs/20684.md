### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of any condition (including '[ASSURE indications] Any secondary malignancies or metastasis' source concepts) for the first time in the person's history.

2. measurement of '[ASSURE indications] Any secondary malignancies or metastasis' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

Restrict entry events to having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Prostate cancer', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.  

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Age >= 18

Entry events with the following event criteria: who are &gt;= 18 years old.

#### 2. Gender=Male

Entry events with the following event criteria: who are male.

#### 3. No more than 1 claim with a diagnosis of other types of cancer before index date

Entry events with at most 1 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 2 condition occurrences of '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having at least 1 measurement of '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   4. having at least 2 measurements of '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.


#### 4. >=1 criteria for having available information for hormone sensitivity assessment

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

      1. having at least 1 drug exposure of '[ASSURE indications] ADT - medical castration (LHRH or GnRH agonists and antagonists)', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.
The person exits the cohort when encountering any of the following events:
1. condition occurrences of 'Diagnosis of hormone resistant malignancy'.
2. measurements of '[ASSURE indications] Raised prostate-specific antigen (PSA)'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 drug exposure of '[ASSURE indications] ADT - medical castration (LHRH or GnRH agonists and antagonists)', starting anytime prior to '[ASSURE indications] Raised prostate-specific antigen (PSA)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of '[ASSURE indications] ADT - non-specific procedure', starting anytime prior to '[ASSURE indications] Raised prostate-specific antigen (PSA)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of '[ASSURE indications] ADT - surgical castration procedures (bilateral)', starting anytime prior to '[ASSURE indications] Raised prostate-specific antigen (PSA)' start date; allow events outside observation period.

   5. with at least 2 of the following criteria:


      1. with any of the following criteria:

         1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of '[ASSURE indications] ADT - surgical castration procedures (CPTs that require modifier codes)', starting anytime prior to '[ASSURE indications] Raised prostate-specific antigen (PSA)' start date; allow events outside observation period; with modifier: "left side (used to identify procedures performed on the left side of the body)".
         2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of '[ASSURE indications] ADT - surgical castration procedures (left)', starting anytime prior to '[ASSURE indications] Raised prostate-specific antigen (PSA)' start date; allow events outside observation period.

      2. with any of the following criteria:

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
