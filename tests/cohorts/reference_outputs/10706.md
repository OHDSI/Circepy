### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Muscle weakness'.

2. observations of 'Muscle weakness'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has no events in prior 'clean window' - 180 days

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Muscle weakness', starting in the 180 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no observations of 'Muscle weakness', starting in the 180 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 3 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
