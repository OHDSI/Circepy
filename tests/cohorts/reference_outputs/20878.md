### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Urinary Tract Infection'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has no UTI in prior 90 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Urinary Tract Infection', starting in the 90 days prior to cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
