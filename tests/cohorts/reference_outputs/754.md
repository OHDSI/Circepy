### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Down syndrome'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. 2nd down syndrome code within 365 days after index

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Down syndrome', starting between 1 days after and 365 days after cohort entry start date; having no condition eras of 'Pregnancy Condition', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Down syndrome' start date and ending between 0 days after and 0 days after 'Down syndrome' start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
