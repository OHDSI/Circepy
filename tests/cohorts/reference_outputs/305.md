### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Neurofibromatosis Syndrome'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No NF2: Anytime in persons history  

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Neurofibromatosis type 2'.

#### 2. No vestibular schwannoma: Anytime in persons history (note, also includes NF2)  

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Vestibular Schwannoma or Neoplasm of acoustic nerve'.

#### 3. No hearing loss: Anytime in persons history  

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hearing loss'.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
