### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of '[EPI1327] Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding - excl. ulcers wo explicit ref to bleeding'.

2. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding - excl. ulcers wo explicit ref to bleeding'.

3. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] GI perforation, fistula, abscess'.

4. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] GI ulcer no malignancy'.

5. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] intestinal obstruction'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Excluding ICD10 without bleed source concepts 

Entry events having no condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] ICD10 non-bleeding source codes' source concepts), starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. Excluded icd10 codes for GI ulcer

Entry events having no condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] Excluded icd10 codes for GI ulcer' source concepts), starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
