### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Invasive Disease due to E.coli - Specific'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific'; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Infection due to E.coli, excluding intestinal infections', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific' start date; at same visit as 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific'.
   2. having no condition occurrences of 'Selected non-E. coli gram-negative infections', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific' start date; at same visit as 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific'.
   3. having no condition occurrences of 'Select gram positive bacteria', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific' start date; at same visit as 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific'.
   4. having no condition occurrences of 'Select Fungal Infections', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific' start date; at same visit as 'Invasive Disease, unspecified - Specific'.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
