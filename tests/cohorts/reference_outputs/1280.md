### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Small Cell Lung Cancer'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Primary Malignant neoplasm of lung'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Small cell carcinoma of unspecified site', starting between 1 days before and 1 days after 'Primary Malignant neoplasm of lung' start date; allow events outside observation period.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
