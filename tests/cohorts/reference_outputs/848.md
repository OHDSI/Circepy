### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 365 days before event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of '[HowOften] Primary adenocarcinoma of rectum' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No exposure to medical oncological treatment for colorectal cancer

Entry events with at most 0 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 drug exposure of '[HowOften] Drug used in colorectal cancer', starting  180 days before cohort entry start date.


#### 2. No exposure to radiation oncological treatment for colorectal cancer

Entry events with at most 0 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of '[HowOften] Radiation therapy', starting  180 days before cohort entry start date.


#### 3. No potential curative rectal resection

Entry events with at most 0 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of '[HowOften] Potential curative rectal resections for rectal cancer', starting  180 days before cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
