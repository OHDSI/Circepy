### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Pityriasis rubra pilaris, inflammatory dermatosis, eruption, psoriasis'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Pityriasis rubra pilaris' for the first time in the person's history, starting between 0 days before and 30 days after 'Pityriasis rubra pilaris, inflammatory dermatosis, eruption, psoriasis' start date.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
