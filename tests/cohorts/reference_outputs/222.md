### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Stevens-Johnson syndrome or TEN'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Visit is Inpatient or Emergency Room

Entry events having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Emergency room or Inpatient Visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after cohort entry start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the all events per person.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
