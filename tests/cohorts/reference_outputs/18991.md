### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'ADHD predominantly inattentive type' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Must NOT have hyperactive-type ADHD diagnosis on index date

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'ADHD hyperactive impulsive type', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
