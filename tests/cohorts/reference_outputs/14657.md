### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'ICANS Symptoms (narrow) '.

2. condition occurrences of 'ICANS standards [NEED UPDATE WITH NEW VOCAB FEB 2024]
'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Two codes for diagnosis of ICANS occurring after a diagnosis of any malignant neoplasm (within 180 days) [two codes could be on the same day]  or a two diagnosis of ICANS symptoms occurring within 30 days post a CAR-T and T-cell therapy procedures.

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 2 condition occurrences of any condition (including 'ICANS source codes' source concepts), starting between 0 days after and 0 days after cohort entry start date; having at least 1 condition era of 'malignant neoplasm', starting between 180 days before and 0 days before any condition start date.
   2. having at least 2 condition occurrences of 'ICANS Symptoms (narrow) ', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; with any of the following criteria:

      1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'CAR-T and T-cell therapy procedures', starting between 30 days before and 0 days after 'ICANS Symptoms (narrow) ' start date.
      2. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'CAR-T and T-cell therapy drugs', starting between 30 days before and 0 days after 'ICANS Symptoms (narrow) ' start date.



### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
