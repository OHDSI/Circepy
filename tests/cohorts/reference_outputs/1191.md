### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'CKD condition codes from literature'.

2. condition occurrences of '[LEGEND HTN] Chronic kidney disease'.

3. condition occurrences of 'End stage renal disease (LEGEND HTN and addtional codes)'.

4. procedure occurrences of 'End stage renal disease (LEGEND HTN and addtional codes)'.

5. observations of 'End stage renal disease (LEGEND HTN and addtional codes)'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
