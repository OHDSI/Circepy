### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 365 days before event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Acute Hepatic Failure'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Prior Liver Disease

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Viral hepatitis', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Chronic Hepatic Failure', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   3. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Liver Cirrhosis', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   4. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Liver Fibrosis', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   5. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Toxic Liver Disease', starting anytime up to 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   6. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Hepatic Necrosis', starting anytime up to 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   7. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Disorder caused by alcohol', starting between 365 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   8. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Alcoholic hepatitis', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   9. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Steatosis of liver, not including non-alcoholic', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   10. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Malignancies that spread to liver (breast, esophageal, stomach, pancreatic, lung, kidney, melanoma)', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   11. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Chronic Liver Disease', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   12. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Ascites', starting anytime up to 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   13. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Portal Hypertension', starting anytime up to 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   14. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Liver Transplant', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   15. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Cholestasis', starting anytime up to 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   16. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Non-Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   17. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Autoimmune Hepatitis', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   18. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Primary Biliary Cholangitis', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   19. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Hemochromatosis', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   20. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Wilsons disease', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   21. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Neoplasm of liver', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.
   22. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Other Liver problems', starting anytime up to 7 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
