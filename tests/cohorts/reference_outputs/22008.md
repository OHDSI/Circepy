### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. drug eras of 'Assure Exposure - C -  adalimumab'.

2. drug eras of 'cyclosporine, methotrexate, acitretin, azathioprine, or fumarate'.

3. drug eras of 'IL12/IL23 inhibitors (ustekinumab)'.

4. drug eras of 'IL23 ibhibitors (risankizumab, guselkumab, tildrakizumab)'.

5. drug eras of 'TNF inhibitors (adalimumab, certolizumab pegol, etanercept)'.

6. procedure occurrences of 'phototherapy'.

7. drug eras of 'Assure Exposure - C -  apremilast'.

8. drug eras of 'deucravacitinib'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Plaque psoriasis prior to index

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Plaque psoriasis', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date.

#### 2. 365d Observable Time

Entry events having at least 1 observation period, starting anytime up to 365 days before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
