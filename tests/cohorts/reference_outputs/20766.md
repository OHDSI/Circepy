### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Anemia'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No hereditary anemia

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hereditary anemia', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

#### 2. no measurements on same day with value above diagnostic criteria

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no measurements of 'Expanded Hemoglobin Measurement', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value &gt;= 14; unit: "g/dl", "gram per deciliter" or "gram per deciliter calculated".
   2. having no measurements of 'Hematocrit', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value &gt; 41; unit: "percent".


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 365 days of each other.
