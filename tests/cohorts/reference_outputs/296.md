### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Transverse Myelitis'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Symptoms for Transverse Myelitis'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Transverse Myelitis', starting between 0 days after and 30 days after 'Symptoms for Transverse Myelitis' start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 180 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 180 days of each other.
