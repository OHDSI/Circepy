### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of 'Measurement/Procedure required for diagnosis of condition'.

2. measurements of 'Measurement/Procedure required for diagnosis of condition'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Excluded conditions

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Excluded criteria - any conditions that should be excluded', starting between 0 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
