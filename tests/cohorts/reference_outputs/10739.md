### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Epistaxis'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'epistaxis procedures'.

3. observations of 'epistaxis procedures'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. no epistaxis in last 7 days

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Epistaxis', starting in the 7 days prior to cohort entry start date.
   2. having no procedure occurrences of 'epistaxis procedures', starting in the 7 days prior to cohort entry start date.
   3. having no observations of 'epistaxis procedures', starting in the 7 days prior to cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
