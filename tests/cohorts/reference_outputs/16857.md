### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Acute Myeloid Leukemia, unspecific '.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. have a specified diagnosis of AML

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of '

Acute myeloid leukemia, disease', starting between 0 days after and 365 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
