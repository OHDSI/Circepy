### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. measurements of 'Anti-beta2 glycoprotein 1 antibody'; having at least 1 measurement of 'Anti-beta2 glycoprotein 1 antibody', starting between 90 days after and 365 days after 'Anti-beta2 glycoprotein 1 antibody' start date.

2. measurements of 'Anticardiolipin antibody measurement'; having at least 1 measurement of 'Anticardiolipin antibody measurement', starting between 90 days after and 365 days after 'Anticardiolipin antibody measurement' start date.

3. measurements of 'Lupus anticoagulant assay'; having at least 1 measurement of 'Lupus anticoagulant assay', starting between 90 days after and 365 days after 'Lupus anticoagulant assay' start date.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Anticoagulants within 548 days after first test

Entry events having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Anticoagulants indicated for APS', starting between 0 days before and 548 days after cohort entry start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
