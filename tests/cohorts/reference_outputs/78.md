### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Cardiac arrhythmia composite'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers'.

3. device exposures of 'Procedures/Devices to treat cardiac arrhythmia or pacemakers'.

4. drug exposures of 'Antiarrhythmics'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Limit to inpatient visit

Entry events having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient Visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
