### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Bradycardia or heart block'.

2. device exposure of 'Pacemaker' for the first time in the person's history.

3. procedure occurrence of 'Pacemaker' for the first time in the person's history.

Restrict entry events to having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient Visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.  

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
