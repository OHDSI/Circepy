### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 365 days before event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of '[HowOften] Potential curative colectomies for colon cancer'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Previous colon cancer

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[HowOften] Primary adenocarcinoma of colon' for the first time in the person's history, starting between 365 days before and 30 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
