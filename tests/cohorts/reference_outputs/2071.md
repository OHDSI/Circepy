### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Thyroiditis including hypo and hyperthyroidism'.

2. drug exposures of '

levothyroxine'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Thyroiditis including hypo and hyperthyroidism

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Thyroiditis including hypo and hyperthyroidism', starting between 0 days before and 365 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. Excluding alternative causes of hypothyroidism

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Hypothyroidism other than Hashimoto's Disease', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
