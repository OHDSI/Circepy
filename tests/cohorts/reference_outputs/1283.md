### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Bone Pain'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Bone finding'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Pain finding at anatomical site', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Bone finding' start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
