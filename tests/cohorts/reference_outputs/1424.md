### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'opioid overdose condition'.

2. drug exposures of 'opioid overdose treatment drugs'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 7 days of each other.
