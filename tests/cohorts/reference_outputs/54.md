### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Febrile seizure and unspecified seizure '.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Has febrile seizure diagnosis 

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Febrile seizure', starting between 0 days before and 42 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
