### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of 'PCI'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'CABG'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Hospitalization

Entry events having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Hospitalization', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
