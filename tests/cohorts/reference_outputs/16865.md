### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Interstitial cystitis'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No OAB on the index date

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Overactive Bladder', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. No bladder cancer prior

Entry events having no condition occurrences of '[EPI_898] [EPI_899] Bladder cancer (standard and non-standard)', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
