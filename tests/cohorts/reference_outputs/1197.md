### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. 45 day washout period 

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding', starting in the 45 days prior to cohort entry start date.
   2. having no procedure occurrences of 'Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding', starting in the 45 days prior to cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
