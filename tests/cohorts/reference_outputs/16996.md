### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Chronic Graft versus host disease' for the first time in the person's history.

2. condition occurrence of 'Graft versus host disease' for the first time in the person's history; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation (HSCT) ', starting anytime up to 180 days before 'Graft versus host disease' start date.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation (HSCT) ', starting anytime up to 180 days after 'Graft versus host disease' start date.
   3. having at least 1 observation of 'Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation (HSCT) ', starting anytime up to 180 days after 'Graft versus host disease' start date.


Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
