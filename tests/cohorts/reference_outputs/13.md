### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Myalgia Muscle Pain'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Exclude persons with muscle pain that may be explained by injury, ischemia or chronic systemic inflammation

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Secondary explanations for Muscle pain explained by injury, ischemia or systemic inflammation', starting between 3 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
