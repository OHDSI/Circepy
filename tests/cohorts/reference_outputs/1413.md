### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. observations of 'Screening, Brief Intervention, and Referral to Treatment (SBIRT)'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'Screening, Brief Intervention, and Referral to Treatment (SBIRT)'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
