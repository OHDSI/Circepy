### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'ADHD condition or procedure'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'ADHD condition or procedure'.

3. drug exposures of 'ADHD medications'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'ADHD condition or procedure', starting between 0 days before and 365 days after 'ADHD medications' start date.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'ADHD condition or procedure', starting between 0 days before and 365 days after 'ADHD medications' start date.


Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
