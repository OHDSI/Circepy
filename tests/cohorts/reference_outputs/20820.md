### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Leukopenia'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. no conflicting (normal) labs on the same day: 
  

Entry events having no measurements of '

Leukocytes ', starting between 1 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value between 4 and 20; unit: "thousand per microliter", "thousand per cubic millimeter" or "billion per liter".

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 183 days of each other.
