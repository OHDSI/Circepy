### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'COVID-19', starting after December 1, 2019; having at least 1 measurement of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', starting between 0 days after and 3 days after 'COVID-19' start date; with value as concept: "detected", "positive", "positive", "present", "present" or "detected".

2. measurements of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', with value as concept: "detected", "positive", "positive", "present", "present" or "detected"; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'COVID-19', starting between 0 days after and 3 days after 'SARS-CoV-2 test' start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
