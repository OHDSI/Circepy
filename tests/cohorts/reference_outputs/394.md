### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Gastroesophageal reflux disease'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Esophageal reflux finding'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Gastritis', starting between 365 days before and 1 days after 'Esophageal reflux finding' start date.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Esophagitis', starting between 365 days before and 1 days after 'Esophageal reflux finding' start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 180 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
