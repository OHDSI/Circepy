### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Gastritis'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No infectious gastritis events around index

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] Infectious Gastritis v2', starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having no condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] Infectious Gastritis v2' source concepts), starting between 7 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
