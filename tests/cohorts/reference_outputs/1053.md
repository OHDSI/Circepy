### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 365 days before event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. drug exposure of '[HowOften] GLP-1 receptor antagonists' for the first time in the person's history.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. nested in type 2 diabetes mellitus

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of '[PL] Type 2 diabetes mellitus (diabetes mellitus excluding T1DM and secondary)', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of '[PL] Type 1 Diabetes Mellitus', starting anytime on or before '[PL] Type 2 diabetes mellitus (diabetes mellitus excluding T1DM and secondary)' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no condition occurrences of '[PL] Secondary diabetes mellitus', starting anytime on or before '[PL] Type 2 diabetes mellitus (diabetes mellitus excluding T1DM and secondary)' start date; allow events outside observation period.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be based on a continuous exposure to '[HowOften] GLP-1 receptor antagonists':
allowing 90 days between exposures, adding 0 days after exposure ends, and using days supply and exposure end date for exposure duration.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
