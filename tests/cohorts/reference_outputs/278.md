### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Non Chronic Pain or Ache'.

2. observations of 'Non Chronic Pain or Ache'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Non Chronic Pain

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Chronic Pain', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 365 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
