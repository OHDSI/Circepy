### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Chronic inflammatory demyelinating polyradiculoneuropathy'.

2. drug exposures of 'immunoglobulin G'.

3. condition occurrences of 'similar conditions to Chronic inflammatory demyelinating polyradiculoneuropathy'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Requiring Chronic inflammatory demyelinating polyradiculoneuropathy (CIDP) Diagnosis (DX)

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Chronic inflammatory demyelinating polyradiculoneuropathy', starting between 0 days after and 365 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
