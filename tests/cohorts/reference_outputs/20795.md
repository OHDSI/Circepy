### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Herpes zoster'.

2. drug exposures of 'Herpetic antivirals'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Herpes zoster', starting between 1 days after and 14 days after 'Herpetic antivirals' start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 270 days of each other.
