### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Burns'.

2. procedure occurrences of 'Burns'.

3. observations of 'Burns'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. burn treatment in 180 days after index

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Burn drug and procedure treatments', starting between 0 days before and 180 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Burn drug and procedure treatments', starting between 0 days before and 180 days after cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
