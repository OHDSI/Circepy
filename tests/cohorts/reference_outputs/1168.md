### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Skin Ulcer'.

2. observations of 'Skin Ulcer'.

3. procedure occurrences of 'Skin Ulcer'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. 365 washout period

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Skin Ulcer', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date.
   2. having no observations of 'Skin Ulcer', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date.
   3. having no procedure occurrences of 'Skin Ulcer', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
