### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'mild cognitive impairment or disorder (MCI)'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Signs and symptoms of mild-cognitive impairment '; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'mild cognitive impairment or disorder (MCI)', starting between 1 days after and 60 days after 'Signs and symptoms of mild-cognitive impairment ' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having at least 1 observation of 'mild cognitive impairment or disorder (MCI)', starting between 1 days after and 60 days after 'Signs and symptoms of mild-cognitive impairment ' start date; allow events outside observation period.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 90 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
