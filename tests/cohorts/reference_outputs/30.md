### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Respiratory and pulmonary tuberculosis'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. At least 3 distinct drug eras of TB drugs

Entry events having at least 3 distinct standard concepts from drug eras of 'All TB drugs', starting between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 365 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
