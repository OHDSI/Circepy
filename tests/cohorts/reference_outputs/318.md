### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Acute Hepatic Failure'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. have no chronic hepatic failure on the day of diagnosis 

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Chronic Hepatic Failure
', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 1 days of each other.
