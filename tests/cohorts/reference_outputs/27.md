### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. drug exposures of 'Asthma therapy', who are &lt; 55 years old; having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Asthma therapy', starting between 365 days before and 180 days before 'Asthma therapy' start date; allow events outside observation period.

2. condition occurrences of 'Asthma'.

3. observations of 'Asthma'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No Chronic Obstructive Lung Disease (COPD) prior

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Chronic Obstructive Lung Disease', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.
   2. having no drug exposures of 'Long-acting muscarinic antagonists (LAMAs)', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
