### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Fever (38.0°C or higher) pre-coordinated measurement and conditions'.

2. measurements of 'Fever (38.0°C or higher) pre-coordinated measurement and conditions'.

3. measurements of 'Fever (38.0°C or higher) measurement and observation', numeric value between 38 and 42; unit: "degree celsius".

4. measurements of 'Fever (38.0°C or higher) measurement and observation', numeric value between 100.4 and 120; unit: "degree fahrenheit".

5. observations of 'Fever (38.0°C or higher) measurement and observation', numeric value between 38 and 42; unit: "degree celsius".

6. observations of 'Fever (38.0°C or higher) measurement and observation', numeric value between 100.4 and 120; unit: "degree fahrenheit".

7. condition occurrences of 'Cough'.

8. condition occurrences of 'Malaise or Fatigue or (Malaise and Fatigue)'.

9. condition occurrences of 'Altered Smell or Taste'.

10. condition occurrences of 'Dyspnea'.

11. condition occurrences of 'Myalgia'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
