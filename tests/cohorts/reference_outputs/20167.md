### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 30 days after event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Condition of interest'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Inclusion criteria

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Drug indicative of condition', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

   2. with all of the following criteria:

      1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Condition of interest' for the first time in the person's history, starting between 0 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date.
      2. having at least 2 condition occurrences of 'Condition of interest', starting between 0 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date.


#### 2. Exclusion criteria

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Excluded criteria - any conditions that should be excluded - these should all go into excluded covariates in the PheValuator run', starting anytime up to 14 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
