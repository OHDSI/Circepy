### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Dyspnea'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No prior Dyspnea in 30 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Dyspnea', starting in the 30 days prior to cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
