### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Serious Infections '; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or ER visit', starting anytime on or before 'Serious Infections ' start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after 'Serious Infections ' start date.
   2. having at least 1 death of any form, starting between 0 days before and 30 days after 'Serious Infections ' start date.


2. condition occurrences of 'Opportunistic Infections with tuberculosis'.

3. condition occurrences of 'Other infections of interest'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 90 days of each other.
