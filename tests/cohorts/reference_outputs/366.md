### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of ' Streptococcal throat infection'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Pharyngitis'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Streptococcal infectious disease', starting between 1 days before and 1 days after 'Pharyngitis' start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
