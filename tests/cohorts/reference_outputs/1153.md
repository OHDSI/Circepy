### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Seizure'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has no events in prior 'clean window' - 42 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Seizure', starting in the 42 days prior to cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 1 day.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
