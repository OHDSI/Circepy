### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'c difficile - condition'.

2. measurements of 'c difficile - measurement', with value as concept: "positive", "positive for", "positive/elevated", "positive/elevated", "positive/elevated", "positive for c. difficile glutamate dehydrogenase and negative for toxins a+b", "positive for c. difficile glutamate dehydrogenase and toxins a+b" or "positive/elevated".

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No c diff (condition) in history

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'c difficile - condition', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date.

#### 2. No c diff (by measurement) in history

Entry events having no measurements of 'c difficile - measurement', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date; with value as concept: "positive for c. difficile glutamate dehydrogenase and negative for toxins a+b", "positive for c. difficile glutamate dehydrogenase and toxins a+b", "positive", "positive for", "positive/elevated", "positive/elevated", "positive/elevated", "positive/elevated" or "positive/elevated".

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
