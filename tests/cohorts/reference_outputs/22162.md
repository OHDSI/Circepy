### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of '[EPI1327] Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding - excl. ulcers wo explicit ref to bleeding '.

2. procedure occurrences of '[EPI1327] Gastrointestinal hemorrhage GI bleeding - excl. ulcers wo explicit ref to bleeding '.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Excluding ICD10 without bleeding source concepts: Due to a SNOMED mapping issue  

Entry events having no condition occurrences of any condition (including '[EPI1327] ICD10 non-bleeding source codes' source concepts), starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 7 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 30 days of each other.
