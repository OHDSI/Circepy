### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'HDFN - Mother and child codes'; having at least 1 condition era of 'Pregnancy Codes', starting anytime on or before 'HDFN - Mother and child codes' start date and ending  14 days before 'HDFN - Mother and child codes' start date; containing = 0 occurrences.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's start date plus 270 days.
The person exits the cohort when encountering any of the following events:
1. condition occurrences of 'Pregnancy End points'.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 90 days of each other.
