### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Multiple myeloma diagnoses (excl in remission codes)'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. any malignancy before the index

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'any malignancy', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
