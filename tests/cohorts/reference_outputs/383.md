### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Inflammatory dermatosis verbatim', starting on or after October 1, 2015; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Infection of skin', starting between 7 days before and 0 days after 'Inflammatory dermatosis verbatim' start date.
   2. having no condition occurrences of 'Contact Dermatitis', starting between 7 days before and 0 days after 'Inflammatory dermatosis verbatim' start date.
   3. having at least 1 drug exposure of 'Coritcosteroids', starting between 0 days before and 1 days after 'Inflammatory dermatosis verbatim' start date.


2. condition occurrences of 'Eczema but not Inflammatory hyperkeratotic dermatosis'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
