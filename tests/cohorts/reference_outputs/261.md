### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Non ST Elevated Myocardial Infarction'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Acute Myocardial Infarction'; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of 'Non ST Elevated Myocardial Infarction', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Acute Myocardial Infarction' start date; allow events outside observation period.
   2. having no condition occurrences of 'ST elevated Myocardial Infarction', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Acute Myocardial Infarction' start date.
   3. having no condition occurrences of 'ST segment elevation', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after 'Acute Myocardial Infarction' start date.


Restrict entry events to having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient or ER visit', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date and ending between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.  

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
