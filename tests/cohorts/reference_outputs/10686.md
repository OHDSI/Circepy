### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrence of 'Graft versus host disease included non standard' for the first time in the person's history.

2. condition occurrence of any condition (including 'Graft versus host disease included non standard' source concepts) for the first time in the person's history.

3. condition occurrence of any condition (including 'Unspecified graft vs host dx - non standard' source concepts) for the first time in the person's history; having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation (HSCT) ', starting anytime up to 180 days before any condition start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
