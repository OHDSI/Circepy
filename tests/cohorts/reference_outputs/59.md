### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'COVID-19', starting after December 1, 2019.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No SARS-Cov-2 test

Entry events having no measurements of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', starting between 3 days before and 3 days before cohort entry start date; starting after December 1, 2019.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
