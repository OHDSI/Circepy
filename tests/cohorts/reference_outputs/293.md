### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Acute hepatic injury'.

2. condition occurrences of 'Unspecified jaundice'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 visit occurrence of 'Inpatient Setting Concepts', starting anytime on or before 'Unspecified jaundice' start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after 'Unspecified jaundice' start date.
   2. having at least 1 observation of 'Inpatient Setting Concepts', starting anytime on or before 'Unspecified jaundice' start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after 'Unspecified jaundice' start date.
   3. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Inpatient Setting Concepts', starting anytime on or before 'Unspecified jaundice' start date and ending between 0 days after and all days after 'Unspecified jaundice' start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 90 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
