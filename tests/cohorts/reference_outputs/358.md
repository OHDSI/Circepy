### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Respiratory Failure'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No Chronic Respiratory Failure in past 365 days

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Chronic Respiratory Failure', starting between 365 days before and 1 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
