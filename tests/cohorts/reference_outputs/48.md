### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'COVID-19', starting after December 1, 2019; with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no measurements of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', starting between 0 days before and 3 days after 'COVID-19' start date; with value as concept: "detected", "detected", "positive", "positive", "present" or "present".
   2. having at least 1 measurement of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', starting between 0 days before and 3 days after 'COVID-19' start date; with value as concept: "negative", "not detected", "not detected in pooled specimen", "absent", "negative", "not detected" or "absent".


Restrict entry events to having no measurements of 'SARS-CoV-2 test', starting anytime on or before cohort entry start date; starting after December 1, 2019; with value as concept: "detected", "detected", "positive", "positive", "present" or "present".  

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
