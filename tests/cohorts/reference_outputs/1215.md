### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)'.

2. measurements of '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)'.

3. measurements of any measurement (including '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)' source concepts).

4. observations of '[ASSURE Indications] Any cancer (excl. prostate cancer and benign cancers)'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
