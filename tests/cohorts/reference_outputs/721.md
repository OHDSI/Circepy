### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Vision Loss'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Washout - no prior vision loss in 365 days prior: When no vision loss has occurred in past 365 days, then a new code for vision loss may be considered new onset or sudden  

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Vision Loss', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date.

#### 2. No eye pathology

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Eye Pathology', starting in the 365 days prior to cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 90 days of each other.
