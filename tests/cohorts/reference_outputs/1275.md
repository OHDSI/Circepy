### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Nodule of lung'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Multiple Lung Nodule

Entry events with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Multiple nodules of lung', starting between 0 days after and 30 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Multiple lesions', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 730 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
