### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of 'Endometriosis related laproscopic procedures (Prevalent procedures for 1 endo dx cohort)'; having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Endometriosis', starting between 30 days before and 30 days after 'Endometriosis related laproscopic procedures (Prevalent procedures for 1 endo dx cohort)' start date.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. has 2 or more endometriosis diagnoses

Entry events having at least 2 condition occurrences of 'Endometriosis', starting between 0 days before and all days after cohort entry start date.

#### 2. is female

Entry events with the following event criteria: who are female.

#### 3. is aged 15-49

Entry events with the following event criteria: who are between 15 and 49 years old.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
