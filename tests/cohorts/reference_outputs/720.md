### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Aplastic anemia'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No Bone Marrow Hyperplasia, Infiltration including Lymphoma, Leukemia

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Bone Marrow Hyperplasia, Infiltration including Lymphoma, Leukemia', starting between 365 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 2. Not pure red cell aplasia: https://github.com/OHDSI/Vocabulary-v5.0/issues/865  

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no condition occurrences of any condition (including 'Pure Red Cell Aplasia ICD9 CM' source concepts), starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.
   2. having no condition occurrences of 'Pure Red Cell Aplasia', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
