### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. visit occurrences of 'Emergency room'.

2. visit occurrences of 'Main Visit Categories'; with any of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 observation of 'Emergent or Urgent care concepts', starting anytime on or before 'Main Visit Categories' end date and starting between 0 days after and all days after 'Main Visit Categories' start date.
   2. having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Emergent or Urgent care concepts', starting anytime on or before 'Main Visit Categories' start date and starting between 0 days after and all days after 'Main Visit Categories' start date.


3. procedure occurrences of 'Emergent or Urgent care concepts'.

4. observations of 'Emergent or Urgent care concepts'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
