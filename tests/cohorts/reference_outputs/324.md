### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Pain or Ache'.

2. observations of 'Pain or Ache'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No evidence of no pain

Entry events with all of the following criteria:

   1. having no observations of 'Pain or Ache', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; numeric value = 0.
   2. having no observations of 'Pain or Ache', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; with value as concept: "negative", "0 - absent", "none / absent", "absent", "not detected/negative" or "negative".


### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 30 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
