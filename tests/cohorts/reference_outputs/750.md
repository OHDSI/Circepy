### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Pulmonary Hypertension group 2 and general Pulmonary Hypertension'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. Must have Left heart disease : within last 365 days  

Entry events having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Left Heart Disease (for Pulmonary Hypertension group 2)', starting between 365 days before and 7 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

#### 2. Must have undegone Echocardiography or RHC: within last 365 days  

Entry events having at least 1 procedure occurrence of 'Echocardiography & Right Heart Catheterization', starting between 365 days before and 0 days before cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
