### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Iron deficiency Anemia'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No normal low Hemoglobin measurement in blood on index date

Entry events having no measurements of 'Hemoglobin measurement', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date; allow events outside observation period; numeric value between 11 and 14; unit: "g/dl", "gram per deciliter" or "gram per deciliter calculated".

#### 2. No Polycythemia on index date

Entry events having no condition occurrences of 'Polycythemia', starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 365 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
