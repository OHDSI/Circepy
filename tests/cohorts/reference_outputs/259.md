### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'All cause Anaphylaxis'.

2. observations of 'All cause Anaphylaxis'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 14 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
