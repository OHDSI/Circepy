### Cohort Entry Events

People with continuous observation of 180 days before event may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. procedure occurrences of 'Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation (HSCT)'.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. No prior HSCT

Entry events having no procedure occurrences of 'Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation (HSCT)', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date.

Limit qualifying entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
