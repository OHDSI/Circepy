### Cohort Entry Events

People may enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. condition occurrences of 'Multiple myeloma diagnoses (excl in remission codes)'.

Limit cohort entry events to the earliest event per person.

### Inclusion Criteria

#### 1. MM relapse

Entry events with at most 0 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 condition occurrence of 'Multiple myeloma diagnoses (excl in remission codes)' (including 'ICD10CM source codes MM relapse' source concepts), starting between 0 days before and 0 days after cohort entry start date.


#### 2. exclude drugs prior the index

Entry events with at most 0 of the following criteria:

   1. having at least 1 drug era of 'anti MM drugs', starting anytime prior to cohort entry start date.


### Cohort Exit

The person exits the cohort at the end of continuous observation.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 0 days of each other.
