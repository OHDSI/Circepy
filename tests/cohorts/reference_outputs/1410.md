### Cohort Entry Events

People enter the cohort when observing any of the following:

1. measurements of 'use of substance use screening tools'.

2. observations of 'use of substance use screening tools'.

3. procedure occurrences of 'use of substance use screening tools'.

### Cohort Exit

The cohort end date will be offset from index event's end date plus 0 days.

### Cohort Eras

Remaining events will be combined into cohort eras if they are within 1 days of each other.
